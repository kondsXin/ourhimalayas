2022年5月22日下午4点，以美国总统身份首次访日的拜登抵达日本横须贺美军基地。
 
At 4 pm on May 22, 2022, Biden first visited to Japan as President of the United States, arrived at the US military base in Yokosuka, Japan.
 
拜登总统本次日本之行的一个重要议题就是宣布启动引人注目的、筹备了半年之久的“印太经济框架（IPEF）”新机制，
 
One of the key themes of President Biden’s trip to Japan was to announce the launch of a striking new mechanism, the Indo-Pacific Economic Framework (IPEF), which has been in preparation for six months，
 
该议题聚焦于强化半导体研发、芯片制造、供应链安全，建立新的贸易规则等方面。
 
The topic focuses on strengthening semiconductor research and development, chip manufacturing, supply chain security, and the establishment of new trade rules.
 
5月23日，美国总统拜登、日本首相岸田文雄与来自印度、韩国、澳大利亚、文莱、印度尼西亚、马来西亚、新西兰、菲律宾、新加坡、泰国和越南共13个印太地区国家领导人在东京共同宣布成立了新的印太经济同盟，建立了全新的印太经济框架，这13个国家的经济总量约占世界的40%。
 
On May 23rd, US President Joe Biden, Japanese Prime Minister Fumio Kishida and the leaders of 13 Indo-Pacific countries from India, South Korea, Australia, Brunei, Indonesia, Malaysia, New Zealand, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam announced in Tokyo the formation of a new Indo-Pacific Economic Alliance (IPEA), creating a new Indo-Pacific economic framework, with these 13 countries accounting for approximately 40% of the world’s economy.
 
该组织希望就数字经济、供应链、脱碳和适用于工人的法规制定国际规则。
 
The organization wants to create international rules on the digital economy, supply chains, the decarbonisation and regulations that apply to workers.
 
IPEF的核心目标是协调并确保供应链的安全稳定，成员国都希望这是一个长远的经济合作体，而不仅仅只是为了遏制中共对全球供应链的威胁。
 
The core objective of the IPEF is to coordinate and ensure the security and stability of the supply chain, and the member states share the desire for this to be a long-term economic partnership, not just to deter the CCP’s threat to the global supply chain.
