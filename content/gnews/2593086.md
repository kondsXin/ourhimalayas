What exactly is a bear market?
究竟什么是熊市？
 
It’s when stocks drop 20% from a recent high
它是当股票从近期高点下跌 20% 时
 
When we talk about the overall stock market
当我们谈论整体股市时
 
we usually look at a broad index like the s&p 500
我们通常会查看标准普尔500等广泛的指数
 
But individual stocks or commodities can also fall into a bear market if they declined 20%
但个股或大宗商品如果下跌20%，那么市场也可能陷入熊市
 
A bear market is different from a correction
熊市不同于修正
 
That’s a 10% decline
这是 10% 的下降
 
So if the s&p falls into a bear market
所以如果标普跌入熊市
 
it’s already had a correction and then fallen another 10%
它已经进行了修正，然后又下跌了 10%
 
Since World War Two, there have been 14 bear markets for the s&p 500
自第二次世界大战以来，标准普尔 500 指数已经出现了 14 次熊市
 
A bear market doesn’t always signal a severe economic downturn in the last 40 years
熊市并不总是预示着过去 40 年出现严重的经济衰退
 
for bear markets have occurred without a recession
因为熊市也会在没有衰退的情况下发生
 
but the drops that coincide with recessions tend to be worse
但与衰退同时出现的跌幅往往更糟
 
The good news is bear markets don’t tend to last as long as bull markets
