## 加拿大保守党领袖候选人波利耶夫提出《防止政府强制接种疫苗法》

作者：加拿大温哥华扬帆农场 动物农庄里的小蚂蚁[Michael.Tonny](https://gnews.org/zh-hans/author/michaeltonny/)（专栏） Gettr: [@Michael\_tonny89](https://gettr.com/user/michael_tonny89)
 
![](https://assets.gnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/michael-4.jpg)
 
6月2日，加拿大保守党领袖候选人皮埃尔·波利耶夫（Pierre Poilievre）提出《防止政府强制接种疫苗法》（BILL C-278：[Prevention of Government-imposed Vaccination Mandates Act](https://www.parl.ca/DocumentViewer/en/44-1/bill/C-278/first-reading)），该法案将结束当前的联邦疫苗强制接种政策并防止类似强制措施再次发生。
 
![](https://assets.gnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/poilievre-bill-mandates_1654444482.jpg)
 
如果该法案得以通过：
 
要求接种中共病毒(COVID-19)疫苗将不能再作为联邦公共行政部门的雇佣条款或条件，或解雇雇员的权力，或仅仅因为他们没有接种针对COVID-19的疫苗而暂停或减少他们的工资；
 
Subsection 11.‍1(2) of the Financial Administration Act：
 
do not include the power to require as a term or condition of employment in the federal public administration that a person receive a vaccine against the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) or to provide for the termination of an employee, or for a suspension of or a reduction in their pay, solely on the ground that they have not received a vaccine against COVID-19
 
不得要求个人接种中共病毒(COVID-19)疫苗，作为在联邦工作、承接业务或与之交易相关的雇佣条款或条件；
 
Section 157 of the Canada Labour Code ：
 
No regulation may be made under this Part that requires, as a term or condition of employment in or in connection with the operation of a federal work, undertaking or business, that a person receive a vaccine against the coro­navirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).
 
航空部门以防止中共病毒(COVID-19)的引入或传播而制定的任何法规、通知、命令、安全措施或紧急指示，均不得以他们没有接种过针对这种疾病的疫苗作为唯一理由，禁止或具有禁止人员进入飞机的效果；
 
The Aeronautics Act：
 
No regulation, notice, order, security measure or emergency direction made under this Part to prevent the introduction or spread of the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) may prohibit or have the effect of prohibiting a person from entering an aircraft on the sole ground that they have not received a vaccine against that disease.
 
铁路部门制定的防止中共病毒(COVID-19)的引入或传播的任何法规、命令、紧急指令或其他文书，均不得以他们没有接种过针对这种疾病的疫苗作为单一理由，禁止或具有禁止人员登上铁路设备的效果；
 
The Railway Safety Act：
 
No regulation, order, emergency directive or other instrument made under this Act to prevent the introduction or spread of the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) may prohibit or have the effect of prohibiting a person from boarding railway equipment on the sole ground that they have not received a vaccine against that disease.
 
船运部门为防止中共病毒(COVID-19)的传入或传播而制定的任何规定，均不得以他们没有接种过针对这种疾病的疫苗作为单一理由，禁止或具有禁止登船的效果。
 
Canada Shipping Act, 2001：
 
No regulation made under this section to prevent the introduction or spread of the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) may prohibit or have the effect of prohibiting a person from boarding a vessel on the sole ground that they have not received a vaccine against that disease.‍
 
波利耶夫在下议院表示，该法案将禁止联邦政府将疫苗强制规定作为旅行或就业的条件，如果该法案获得通过，联邦政府将不再能够要求联邦工作人员接种疫苗以保住他们的薪水和工作。它还将禁止联邦政府要求人们必须接种疫苗才能旅行。
 
换句话说，这将允许所有加拿大人，无论他们的个人医疗决定如何，无论他们是否接种了疫苗，都可以继续在联邦部门工作，或乘坐飞机、火车和其他联邦监管的交通工具。
 
分析认为，新民主党-自由党联盟（NDP-Liberal coalition）不太可能支持这一法案。
 
因为就在5月30日，加拿大国会以117票赞成，202票反对，否决了保守党议员梅丽莎·兰茨曼（Melissa Lantsman）5月17日提出的取消对未接种疫苗的加拿大人的旅行禁令（[Opposition Motion：Rules and service levels for travel](https://www.ourcommons.ca/en/parliamentary-business/2022-05-30)）。
 
![](https://assets.gnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/Snipaste_2022-06-05_23-59-47_1654444838.png)
 
梅丽莎·兰茨曼的理由是：(i)加拿大人目前在加拿大机场的等待时间令人无法接受，尽管机场的运力仍在减少，(ii)专家认为目前的限制无效，并导致额外的延误、成本和混乱，以及严重的劳动力短缺，(iii)加拿大的国际盟友已采取行动取消在机场和其他入境点的COVID-19限制，(iv)加拿大正在失去商业和经济机会，众议院呼吁政府立即恢复大流行前的旅行规则和服务水平。
 
(i) Canadians are currently experiencing unacceptable wait times at Canadian airports, even though airports are still operating at reduced capacity, (ii) current restrictions have been cited by experts as ineffective and contributing to additional delays, costs, and confusion, as well as acute labour shortages, (iii) Canada’s international allies have moved to lift COVID-19 restrictions at airports and other points of entry, (iv) Canada is losing business and economic opportunities,the House call on the government to immediately revert to pre-pandemic rules and service levels for travel.
 
特鲁多（Trudeau）、贾格米特·辛格（Jagmeet Singh）和旅游部长奥马尔·阿尔加布拉（Omar Alghabra）投票反对该动议，几乎所有自由党（Liberal）、新民主党（NDP，Nouveau Parti démocratique）和魁北克集团（Bloq Quebecois）成员都投了反对票。
 
波利耶夫的动议可能更多的是为党内竞选服务，因为2022年6月3日美国东部时间11:59，是保守党领袖选举中投票会员资格缴费的截止日期。
